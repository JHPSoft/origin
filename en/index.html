What is industrial automation?

Industrial automation is an innovative technology that automates many processes in manufacturing sites, significantly enhancing productivity.

Concept of industrial automation

Industrial automation refers to automating the production process using machinery or computers.
This technology has brought significant changes in manufacturing operations and is being utilized across various industrial sectors.

Automation System
Industrial automation involves automating the production process using machinery, sensors, and computer technology.
This enables increased productivity and cost savings.
Control and Measurement
Industrial automation encompasses control systems and measurement technologies used to control and monitor production lines.
This enables efficient management of the production process.
Robotics Technology
Industrial automation also includes the adoption of robotics technology.
Robots either replace or assist human workers, enhancing work speed and accuracy.

Benefits of Industrial Automation

Productivity Enhancement
Automated systems lead to cost savings in labor and an increase in productivity.
Ensuring Worker Safety
Automating some hazardous tasks through systems such as robots ensures the safety of workers.
Product Quality Improvement
Minimizing errors leads to an improvement in product quality.
Maintaining and Achieving Productivity Growth
Automation is necessary to maintain and enhance consistent productivity.
